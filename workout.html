"One day or day one."

Why?

I work out for my mind. When I push myself to the limit, almost collapsing from exhaustion, I know I’m growing. Progress over time makes me feel accomplished—my mind thrives, my body shows it, and most definitely feels it.

Nutrition/Diet

Eat something good for your muscles two hours before each workout and then immediately after.

Stretching

Make sure to stretch your entire body before your workout.

Workout Plan

Daily

  • Handgrip (3x your choice)

Lower Body (Monday & Wednesday)

  • Squats (3x your choice)
  • Wall Sits (3x your choice)

Upper Body (Tuesday & Thursday)

  • Push Ups (3x your choice)
  • Bench Press (3x your choice)
  • Overhead Press (3x your choice)
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raise (Side & Front) (3x your choice)
  • Dumbbell Rows (3x your choice)
  • Curls (3x your choice)

Recovery

Take a hot bath daily until Saturday. On Saturday, switch to a cold bath to repair your body.
